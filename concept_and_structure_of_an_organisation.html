Types of Structures:

1. Line Structure:

  • Oldest and simplest type
  • Direct authority flow from top to bottom
  • Clear chain of command
  • Fast decision making
  • Example: Small manufacturing units

2. Functional Structure:

  • Based on functions like Marketing, Finance, HR, etc.
  • Each department has its own specialist
  • Clear role definition
  • Example: Big companies with specialized departments

3. Line and Staff Structure:

  • Combination of line authority and staff advisors
  • Line managers take decisions
  • Staff provide expert advice
  • Example: Medium to large organizations

4. Matrix Structure:

  • Dual authority system (functional + project-based)
  • Employees report to more than one manager
  • Example: IT companies, consultancy firms

5. Divisional Structure:

  • Organisation is divided on the basis of products, regions, or customers
  • Each division has its own resources and management
  • Example: Large conglomerates (like Tata Group, Reliance)
